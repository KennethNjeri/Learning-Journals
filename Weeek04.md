## Content Insights
Goal of web design is to create the best information user experiences, 
the modern web design process involves discrete  phases such as Initial consultation and pre-process stage which involves understanding what the uses care about,
content strategy phase which involves creating user personas, coming up with content priority hierarchies , information architecture and wireframes, development phase and testing and launching

## Learning Insights
This week has been most informative in terms of the process that developers follow to come up with websites for their clients. Employers look for people who can translate user insights into clear design decisions, work iteratively within agile workflows and collaborate effectively using shared tools.
To become a strong candidate, I need to have a deep understanding of such processes and tools as to meet client goals and improve user experiences.
